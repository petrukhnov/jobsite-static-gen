Mönchengladbach is a middle size city surrounded by many smaller villages. Therefore the city is optimal in both cases: to live intensive life in the middle of the city or to have it quieter in the countryside. The rents are relatively inexpensive. Borussia Mönchengladbach is one of the most famous and most successful soccer teams in Germany.

In case you want to have even more actions and events than Mönchengladbach offers, you can live in fabulous Dusseldorf. It will take you only 20 minutes to travel from Dusseldorf to our office by car.
